The rules, briefly

Four moves make up every session

🗂️

Pick your category

Five hand-built word sets — Animals, Elements, Foods, Countries, Occupations — each scaling from short and simple to long and challenging.

✅

Submit when ready

Nothing advances until you say so. Arrange your answer, hit Submit to check it, and take your time reviewing a win.

📚

Learn as you play

Every word comes with a brief, accurate explanation revealed once the round ends — solved, timed out, or skipped — so it sticks for next time.

🔥

Score with intent

Every word in a tier pays the same: 10pts easy, 20pts medium, 30pts hard. Hints cost 5 points, skips cost a life, wrong answers cost your streak — no hidden math.
